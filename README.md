# Social Contract

<hr>

### A Villager overhaul mod for Minecraft

Social contract is a mod designed to add more depth to 
Minecraft by making Villagers a bit more intelligent and human.

<hr>

## Features:

- Villagers will need a home, one with a bed, and one that is completley safe from mobs spawning inside
and attackers from the outside. [IMPLEMENTED]
  - Villagers will only achieve high trades if they have a nice, large, and safe home
  - No more Villager trading hall slums.
- Villagers will willingly follow you if you have enough 
reputation with them [IMPLEMENTED]
  - gone are the days of forcibly moving Villagers with
boats and Minecarts.
- You can now give Villagers gifts [IMPLEMENTED]
  - Villagers have a range of items that can be gifted in order
to increase your reputation a bit.
- Librarians have been reworked
  - The Master and Expert tiers of Librarians will offer one Enchanted Book each
  - The books offered are determined by what Books are gifted to the Villager
  - The first book gift locks in the trade, however, higher tier books can be given
  - The level and type of enchantment that a Librarian can give will be determined by how nice of a house it has, and gifts you have given it.
- Villager breeding has been reworked
  - Villagers now require a specific item to be gifted to them in order to breed
  - They are also required to have a decent house.
  - No more Goblin-Hut-esque style breeders.
- Villager Pathfinding has been tweaked 
  - Villagers will now try to avoid pathfinding to dark areas, preferring to stay within light

## Rant

Minecraft has always been a lonely game, that is, if you play singleplayer. Playing with friends turns the game into something else, something more alive. 
Now, only *some* of us have friends, which is a problem if we want to have that 
alive feeling in our world.

The Villager really should have been the mob that felt like a real character in the world,
not exactly a real player, but close enough to add a lot of depth to the world. 

However, Villagers are implemented poorly. Players essentially treat Villagers just like they would
cattle or slaves. Part of this is because most players can't be bothered to build something nice
for Villagers instead of speed running the Ender Dragon. But another, more important part of this
is that Villagers are really stupid and behave quite like cattle indeed. They
run off outside the village and get killed, they get stuck in 1 by 1 holes, 
they don't close the friggin door after themselves sometimes when being chased by a Zombie. Because of this, it
is neccessary that Villagers get a rework to make them the lively NPCs that they should be.
